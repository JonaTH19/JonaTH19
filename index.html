ORIGINS

London coffee shop is a coffee shop inspired by the architecture and envionment in london located in montana South Africa.London coffee shop was first established by MR JR Wenthoeker in 1945 then later passed on to his sons who know own it.When the coffee shop was first established it was made to bring people together and relax their nerves after a long day of hard labour. Till this day London Coffee shop Aims at bringing idividuals together and bringing peace and serenity amongst people.
